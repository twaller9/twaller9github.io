01 — Introduction

What Is MedEdAI?

🩺 Replace with a logo, screenshot, or project image

MedEdAI is a startup building AI-powered clinical simulations for medical education. The core idea is simple but powerful: instead of memorizing static case studies, students interact with realistic patient scenarios driven by large language models, receiving adaptive feedback in real time.

Access to high-quality clinical training is expensive and logistically difficult — MedEdAI aims to democratize it. When I joined the team, the potential felt obvious. The hard part was building it.
